title: Contact templates
---

A contact or a contact template can only inherit one contact template.

### Configuration

To add a contact template:

Go to **Configuration > Users > Contact Templates** and click on **Add**

:::note

Refer to the chapter covering configuration of [contacts](create-users-manually.md). In addition, the contacts templates are used for automatic import of profiles via [LDAP](../administration/authentication/ldap).

:::
